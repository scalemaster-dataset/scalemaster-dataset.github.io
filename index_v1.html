Abstract

We present ScaleMaster Dataset, a comprehensive point cloud dataset designed for multi-scale indoor scene understanding and SLAM research. Our dataset contains 25 diverse sequences captured across various indoor environments including libraries, parking areas, hotel rooms, offices, and multi-floor structures. Each sequence features unique challenges such as low-light conditions, repetitive structures, vertical navigation, and multi-floor loops, making it ideal for benchmarking robust SLAM algorithms.
